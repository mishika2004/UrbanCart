# Shipping Policy — UrbanCart

## Delivery Timeframes
- **Metro cities** (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai): 2–4 business days
- **Tier 2 & 3 cities**: 4–7 business days
- **Remote & rural areas**: 7–10 business days

## Shipping Charges
- Orders above **₹499** — **FREE Delivery** across India
- Orders below ₹499 — Flat ₹49 delivery charge
- Furniture & large items — Additional ₹199 handling fee may apply

## Order Tracking
- Once dispatched, you will receive an SMS and email with your tracking number.
- Orders are typically dispatched within **24 hours** of placement.
- You can track your package using the tracking ID on our logistics partner's website.

## Delivery Attempts
- Our courier partner will attempt delivery up to **3 times**.
- If all attempts fail, the package is returned to our warehouse and a refund is initiated within 5–7 business days.

## Delayed or Lost Shipments
- If your order hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, contact us at **support@urbancart.in** or call **+91 1800 123 456**.
- We will investigate and resolve the issue within 3 business days.
