# Privacy Policy — UrbanCart

## Data We Collect
- **Account information**: name, email address, phone number
- **Shipping addresses**: for delivery purposes
- **Order history**: to track and manage your purchases
- **Usage data**: pages visited, search queries (anonymised)

## How We Use Your Data
- To process and deliver your orders
- To send order confirmation, shipping updates, and invoices
- To improve our product recommendations and shopping experience
- To respond to customer support queries

## Data Sharing
- We **do not sell** your personal data to third parties.
- Data is shared only with:
  - **Payment gateways** for transaction processing
  - **Logistics partners** for delivery
  - **Legal authorities** when required by law

## Cookies
- We use cookies to improve your browsing experience.
- You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

## Data Retention
- Account data is kept as long as your account is active.
- Order data is retained for 5 years for compliance.
- You can request deletion of your data by emailing **support@urbancart.in**.

## Your Rights
- Access your personal data
- Request corrections
- Request data deletion
- Withdraw consent for marketing communications
