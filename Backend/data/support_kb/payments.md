# Payments — UrbanCart

## Accepted Payment Methods
- **UPI** (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
- **Credit/Debit Cards** (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- **Net Banking** (all major Indian banks)
- **Wallets** (Paytm, PhonePe, Amazon Pay)

## Invoice
- An invoice is automatically generated and emailed to you after successful payment.
- You can also download invoices from your Order History page.

## Order Cancellation
- You can cancel your order within **1 hour** of placing it by contacting support.
- Once an order is **dispatched**, cancellation is not possible.
- For cancelled orders, the refund will be processed within 3–5 business days.

## Payment Security
- All transactions are secured with **256-bit SSL encryption**.
- We do **not** store your card details on our servers.
- Payments are processed through certified payment gateways.

## Failed Payments
- If your payment fails but the amount is debited, it will be auto-refunded within **5–7 business days**.
- If the refund does not appear, contact your bank or email us at **support@urbancart.in**.
