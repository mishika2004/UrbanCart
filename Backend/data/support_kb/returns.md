# Returns & Refunds Policy — UrbanCart

## Return Window
- You can return eligible products within **7 days** of delivery.
- Items must be **unused** and in **original packaging**.

## How to Initiate a Return
1. Email **support@urbancart.in** with your Order ID and reason for return.
2. Our team will review and approve within 24 hours.
3. A **free pickup** will be arranged within 24 hours of approval.

## Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** after return approval.
- Amount is credited back to your **original payment method**.

## Non-Returnable Items
The following items are **non-returnable** for hygiene reasons:
- Intimate apparel
- Perishable goods
- Used or washed items

## Exchange Policy
- Exchanges are subject to availability.
- You can request an exchange instead of refund by mentioning it in your return email.

## Damaged or Defective Products
- If you receive a damaged or defective product, contact us immediately.
- We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.
- Please share photos of the damaged item for verification.
